    Long-term Vision
    • Tangibly improve the ability to accurately and efficiently solve the neutral particle transport equation through the development of algorithms and codes.
    • Facilitate the application of these improved tools to novel and interesting problems, in particular those expanding clean energy and growing human knowledge.
    • Train scientists and engineers to conduct computational science with a strong sense of ethics:
      • connection with impact and application;
      • responsibility for correctness and reproducibility;
      • emphasis on openness and transparency.
